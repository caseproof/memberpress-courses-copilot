Enable high contrast mode in your OS settings to test enhanced contrast:

  • Windows: Settings → Ease of Access → High contrast
  • macOS: System Preferences → Accessibility → Display → Increase contrast
  • Chrome: chrome://flags → Force High Contrast Mode
